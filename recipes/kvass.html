Kvass

Description

Kvass is a fermented drink popular in some slav countries and Baltic countries. Pretty much tastes like apple cider mixed with light beer. Also good for cure hangover.

Ingredients

  • 1.5L water
  • 1/3 cup of raisins
  • 1 teaspoon dry yeast
  • 100g sugar
  • 2 slices of black rye or pumpernickel bread
  • 1 lemon
  • hermetic jars for storage
